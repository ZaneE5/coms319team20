Special Interest Clubs

These are some of the special interest clubs avialable at ISU.

A Novel Idea is an on-campus social book club for those who have a love for reading. We read one book a month which is chosen based upon the members original book preferences.

The Gaffer's Guild is dedicated to education and training in the art and science of glassblowing. We offer half-semester long classes to students and community members and have open studio hours for members. We perform demos for the community and school groups and have visits from professionals several times a year. We hold periodic sales to sustain the club and repair equipment.

The Data Science Club is an organization that promotes data science to all students. The goal is to bring together students with interest in data to participate in networking activities, company visits, and technical workshops. Outside of normal club times we also compete in data science competitions and participate in outreach events. We encourage all students with an interest in data science to join our organization!

The Baking Club is a relaxed organization that focuses on baking, building relationships, and community engagement. No baking experience is needed to join!
